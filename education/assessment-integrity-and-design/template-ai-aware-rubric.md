## Performance Dimensions

| Dimension | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Cognitive Quality | Evidence of analysis, evaluation, or synthesis |
| Judgement | Independent decision-making beyond AI output |
| AI Transparency | Clear explanation of how AI was used |
| Ethical Awareness | Recognition of limitations, bias, or uncertainty |
| Accountability | Work can be explained and defended if questioned |

---

## Marker Guidance

Markers should:
- assess reasoning and justification
- value transparency over concealment
- apply criteria consistently across students

Markers should not:
- infer AI use from writing style alone
- penalise responsible, disclosed AI use aligned with design
